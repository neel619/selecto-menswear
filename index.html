Our Story

A Legacy Stitched
Into Every Thread

Founded in 1975 in Mumbai, Selecto Men's Wear began as a vision to bring precision tailoring and premium menswear to the heart of the city crafted under the vision of Abdul Hamid Shaikh. What started as a single shop has grown into one of Mumbai's most trusted names in bespoke clothing.

Over five decades, we have served thousands of clients — from grooms preparing for the biggest day of their lives, to corporate executives who demand perfection, to college students making their mark.

"From fathers to sons, we've stitched memories."

Today, Selecto is a well-known brand across India — blending heritage tailoring techniques with modern fashion sensibilities. Every stitch, every fabric, every fitting is a testament to 50+ years of mastery.
